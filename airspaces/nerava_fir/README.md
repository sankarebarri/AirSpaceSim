# Nerava FIR (fictional training airspace)

A completely fictional mid-Atlantic en-route training sector centred on the
Nerava VOR (`NRV`, 33.5N 41.0W). All fixes, routes, identifiers, and
coordinates are invented for training and are **not operationally valid**.

- `airspace.v1.json` — points, ATS-style routes, and the 60 NM training sector
- `scenarios/mixed_traffic.v1.json` — 25-aircraft free-practice session
- `scenarios/sector_traffic.v1.json` — 4-aircraft beginner sector session

Training and visualisation software only. Not for operational use.
